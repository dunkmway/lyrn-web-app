Some things to remember when teaching the Math Program...

  1. We are the teachers. Use Khan Academy and the in-center worksheets for example problems only.
  2. Keep track of lessons taught here. The student may create an account at home but don't rely on Khan academy to track lesson progress.
  3. That being said lesson colors are as follow:
    • Red : Needs review
    • Yellow : Proficient
    • Green : Mastered
  4. Quick hint! If you click on "Math Program" over there to the right, it will send you to Khan academy for the selected program grade. Pretty sweet huh?
  5. We have forms to send home after every lesson with the student to tell the parent what we worked on in center. That way if the student wants to practice at home they know what lessons to go over.
