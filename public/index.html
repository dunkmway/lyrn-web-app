Your ACT Prep Should be Designed Just for You.

And at LYRN, it is! After finding the subjects that you excel in and the ones that you struggle with, we will adapt our course to your personal goals and needs. We learn from each and every practice question you answer, so we know exactly what you've got a handle on and what you need to improve at all times. We will be realistic with both setting and reaching your goals, and we'll work hard to level you up by improving your understanding. Here at LYRN, we believe in the quality of learning, not just the quantity of learning. We will make sure you really understand and retain the information, instead of just making you memorize facts.

And we don't just shove all the ACT content down your throat either. Instead, we focus on what's going to improve your score the most. You're already good at math? Great, let's see if we can get you a near-perfect math score that will bring up your overall score. You struggle with sentence composition? Well that makes up the majority of the reading section, so let's see if we can improve your understanding there. We'll get you where you need to be, without drilling you on some obscure topic (We're looking at you, radical simplification). When you walk into that proctored room on test day, we know you'll be confident in your new-found skills.
