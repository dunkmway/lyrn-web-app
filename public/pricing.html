Depending on which college your student is trying to apply to and what kind of scholarships they are looking for, the number of point they need to go up in the ACT will differ.

For students that only need 2 to 4 points to reach their goals we reccomend the ACT Fundamentals. The fundamentals are all your student needs to improve but quick improvement comes with some downsides. The same program that helps students to get a quick improvement on their test is also limited by how far they can go without really getting into the nitty gritty. That is why the program takes less time. Any longer and your student won't see improvement. During this time, however, your student will practice, practice, practice! They will know the test better than they have ever known it before and will feel prepared and confident to tackle the real thing!

Students who want to guarantee admission and reap the rewards of thousands of dollars of scholaship funds are facing a much more difficult challenge. A 5 point or more increase is quite the feat for any student and the amount of work required to get there is more than just covering the basics. For our Comprehensive ACT Program, we start from the gound up. We aren't just reviewing key topics that will increase your student's score, we're digging deep and truly understanding the topcis that the ACT is throwing at you. Your student will work hard and our tutors will be right beside them the entire time. Slow and steady progress laying strong foundations is key to reaching a 5+ increase.

Ultimately we are here to help your student achieve their goals. It's not that one program is better than the other, they both play different roles depending on your student's needs. That is why we highly reccomend going through our Initial Consultation first. There we can undersatnd where your student is and set realistic goals based on what colleges your student is interested in. It's also an opportunity for you to try us out before scheduling out a program.

3. Start Your Journey

Take your Lyrn Assessment

The student first takes the Lyrn Assessment (A full length ACT test) and then will recieve a 1-hour test review with one of Lyrn's certified tutors to go over what questions your student missed and give them a feel of our world class tutoring experience.

Meet your Lyrn Specialist

After the Assessment, your student meets their personal Lyrn Specialist in a comprehensive conference. The conference will go over your student's performance on the test, the collegs they want to attend, and the requirements to get in and the scholarships offered. Based on the dreams of your student we will set goals that will help you student reach their potential!

Do the work, feel great!

Over the course of your program, your student will come to the center and meet with a qualified tutor 1 1/2 hours at a time. This is where results start to manifest. A tutoring session typically includes:

  • Grading and reviewing homework
  • Concept reviews
  • Learn 2-3 new concepts
  • Practice test taking
  • Interative and engaged learning
  • Weekly tutoring assignments

Mid-program Assessment

Half way through the program, students take another full length test where they will re-evaluate their progress. Tutors make sure they are on track and that the topics covered thus far have been confidently mastered.

Parents get a progress report to update them on their studen'ts success.
