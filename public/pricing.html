Each of our one-on-one programs comes with a guarantee that if you do all of your homework and come to all of your lessons, we will guarantee that your student will go up that many points on their official ACT test compared to when they first started their program or your money back. See our guarantee policy for more details.

In order for your student to get the best results, our one-on-one programs all end the week before an official ACT test. That way all of the things they learn will be fresh in their minds. We then figure out the start date based on which program you choose and how many times a week you attend lessons. For our set programs, this is typcially 6-8 weeks.

Our ACT Basics program lessons are 1 hour long, and our Guided ACT program lessons are 2 hours long.

We highly recommend 2 lessons a week so that your student has enough time to do their homework and not enough time to forget everything between sessions.

The best approach to learning is 1-on-1. That is why all of our one-on-one programs are just your student and their tutor. That way our tutors can focus soley on your student and they can get the most out of every lesson.

Our lessons are held mainly online over Zoom (we are starting to have in-person lesson either in your home or a public place only in Salt Lake and Utah County UT. Call for more details.) This along with our proprietary software allows for the most convienient and state-of-the-art lessons. We know that online education might not sound the best after the pandemic, but our 1-on-1 tutoring is far superior to classes of 30 students in one meeting.

Every lesson starts off with reviewing the student's homework which is a full section of the ACT. Then in our Guided program the we go over specific topics that appear the most on the test to make the most of the short time we have. Then both programs wrap up with practice from actual ACT questions.
