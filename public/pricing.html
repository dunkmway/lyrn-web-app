FAQ

We can start your program as soon as next week. Not ready to start right now? Give us a call so that we can make sure you are first in line when you're ready. We'll give you a call the week before you would like to start to make sure you can get the time you want before anyone else.

We currently offer 4 options for programs: 2, 4, 6, and 8 week programs. This is made up of 4, 8, 12, and 16 lessons respectively where every 4 lessons we cover the four sections of the ACT: English, Math, Reading, and Science.

Every lessons starts off with reviewing the student's homework. You'll be given homework to do the day after and a week after the lesson to really solidiy those concepts. We'll then teach you the topic that will make the most difference to your ACT score based on how you have been doing in your assignments as well as how frequently the topic appears on the test. After this we'll make sure to practice the topcis we covered by working through real ACT questions.

Lessons are 2 hours long. This gives us time to review what we learned last time as well as go over homework, learn the topics that will make the most difference to your score, and practice the topics we just covered with real ACT questions.

Lessons happen twice a week so that your student has enough time to do their homework and not enough time to forget everything between sessions. We have a Monday Wednesday block and a Tuesday Thursday block you can choose from. Any time slot we have available on these two days we can schedule your lessons. For example, your lessons can be Monday and Wednesday from 5-7 PM.

All of our ACT programs are one-on-one. This guarantees that your tutor is giving you their full attention and every question you have can be answered.

All of our lessons are online. This along with our proprietary software allows for the most convienient and state-of-the-art lessons.

We also offer completely customized programs if the schedule does not work for you or you want to focus more heavily on one section of the test and not others. If you have some questions about how we can personalize our programs even more, give us a call so we can help you to the best of our abilities. See below for our custom program pricing.
