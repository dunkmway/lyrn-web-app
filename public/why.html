Why Lyrn

By Duncan Morais

Just wondering...

December 13, 2021

Revolutionizing education

After years of working in the tutoring industry at different companies and privately tutoring, Matthew and I wanted to make something different. For as long as humans have existed there has been one model to mastery: learn, pratice, repeat. We're not changing that. It has worked for thouasands of years so if it's not broke don't fix it. Instead we're adding the special sauce that every modern company has used to bring their industry into the next level - data. Google, Facebook, Amazon. Revolutionaries in their respective fields. How do we bring that same data to education?

Adaptive Learning Intelligence. We will keep track of every question your student answers in our programs. Every test they take, every worksheet we work through, every lesson we cover will be saved and will affect every step your student takes through our truly custom programs. This is why we have spent thousands of hours developing state-of-the-art software to help your student succeed. The only way to enter the future of education is to use the tools of the future. Our tutors have this technology at their fingers tips and they are eager to help your student reach their dreams.

"The only way to enter the future of education is to use the tools of the future."

Your student isn't the only one learning. The best part about having a data-driven curriculum is that it only gets better and better. Our system will learn and improve with every question your student answers. Constant improvement is key to who we are and we want the same from your student.

Advanced yet simple

We want to simplify the process as much as possible. Yes there is some pretty crazy stuff going on under the hood but at the end of the day if your student doesn't understand what they are learning then we failed. We also don't want to forget about the parents who have to navigate the jungle of options before their student can even get the help they need. That is why we focus on these three things:

  • Tutors not robots - A PhD in astrophysics doesn't mean you can explain high school physics.
  • Transparency - Honesty from the very beginning.
  • Simplicity - Help ease some stress when you need it the most

Our amazing tutors

Our top priority when hiring tutors is their ability to teach. This might sound obvious but a lot of times we see that the knowledge of the tutor is chosen over their ability to teach that knowledge. Don't get me wrong, our tutors are completely prepared to answer any question and teach any concept to your students. We choose to focus on the relationship they build with students and how they can best help your student. Every student is unique and needs a unique approach to learning. This is exactly the need that our tutors fill. Our advanced learning intelligence helps our tutors focus less on what to teach and more about how to teach.

"Every student is unique and needs a unique approach to learning."

Open and Honest

We commit to being completely transparent about our services and pricing. It's all ready stressful enough when you have a student struggling let alone a company hides what they will do and how much it will cost. We're excited about what we offer so why wouldn't we want to share it? If you have any question about our services please contact us and we will be more than happy to help. Transparency will extend throughout your entire time with us. We want you to know how your student is doing and you don't have to wait until the end of the program to hear about it. We are dedicated to keeping in touch with parents the entire time and be willing to help at any time.

This is why we don't have any contracts with our programs. If we aren't providing the service you expected then we still want your student to get the help they need which might mean a different tutor or another service. We want to make it as easy as possible so that your student can get the help they need as quickly as possible and being honest is the best way to do that.

Sweet and Simple

Our goal is to make the tutoring process as simple as possible. Sign up whenever you need and we would love to fit you in. Being online allows us to make the entire process seamless. You can sign up right from our websites and see all of your student's lessons all from your personal dashboard. Anything and everything Lyrn can be found there. No worrying about working with multiple tutors and having to juggle those meetings. We handle the entire process. Your student's tutor will be ready to work as soon as your student logs in to start the session.
