Identify

All 4 answer choices will be nouns, usually pronouns.

Approach

Identify who or what the underlined word is referring to.

  • If it is referencing a noun not found in the sentence, then select the most specific answer choice that identifies it.
  • If it references a noun found in the sentence, select the most specific answer choice that refers to that noun, but check the plurality of the verb connected to the noun and make sure that the noun matches.
